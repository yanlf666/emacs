	/* TACLR  : Timer_A clear.

	/* CM	  : Capture mode.rising edge,falling edge, 
	   CCIS	  : Capture/compare input select.CCIxA, CCIxB, GND, VCC
	   SCS	  : Synchronize capture source.
	   	    0b=Asynchronous capture, b=Synchronous capture 
	   SCCI	  : Synchronized capture/compare input.
	   CAP 	  : Capture mode.0b=Compare mode, b=Capture mode
	   OUTMOD : Output mode. OUTMOD_, OUTMOD_2,...OUTMOD_7
	   CCIE	  : Capture/compare interrupt enable.0b=disabled, b=enabled
	   CCI	  : Capture/compare input.
	   OUT 	  : Output. 0b=Output low, b=Output high.
	   COV	  : Capture overflow.0b=No,b=Capture overflow occurred.
	   CCIFG  : Capture/compare interrupt flag. 0b=No, b=Interrupt pending */"
